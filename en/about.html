Who We Are

About RW

An independent advisory firm defined by integrity, expertise, and a commitment to exceptional outcomes for our clients.

Founded in 1997

Over a Quarter Century of Independent Advisory

RW Capital y Estrategia was founded on a conviction: that companies deserve truly independent financial advice, free from the conflicts inherent in larger institutions. Since 1997, that conviction has guided every client engagement.

We have completed over 100 transactions across M&A, capital raising, debt advisory, valuations, and financial restructuring — serving clients in financial services, technology, industrials, education, and beyond.

Integrity

We provide candid, unbiased advice. Our independence means we always put our clients' interests first — always.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in analysis, execution, and communication. Mediocrity is not an option.

Partnership

We work as true partners to our clients — deeply invested in their success and available throughout the process and beyond.
