Collaboration Models

Five structures. One tailored relationship.

01
Referral Partner
10–20% of fee

You refer the opportunity. RW handles full execution. Ideal for contacts who identify deals but lack the capacity to execute them.

02
Relationship Partner
20–35% of fee

You maintain the primary client relationship. RW leads the process. Your role is liaison and oversight; technical execution is ours.

03
Execution Partner
35–50% of fee

Active participation in the process. You and RW co-manage execution with clearly divided roles. For profiles with solid transactional experience.

04
Strategic Partner
50–65% of fee

You lead the mandate. RW provides brand, technical resources and network access. Designed for senior advisors with a proven track record.

05
Joint Venture
50/50

Full co-advisory. Both parties co-lead the mandate with equal responsibilities and fees. For transactions where both parties' expertise is equally critical.
